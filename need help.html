How to Use This Website

Your cosmic guide to navigating Unbox Me: Beyond the Binary

Exploring the Galaxy

1

Discover Myths & Truths

Start with our "Galaxy of Myths" section to uncover common stereotypes and their factual debunking. Each card flips to reveal the truth behind gender misconceptions.

Pro Tip: Hover over cards to see them glow - this indicates interactive elements!
2

Meet Role Models

In "Stories of the Stars", click on any profile image to be taken to detailed biographies of people who broke gender barriers in their fields.

3

Take the Pledge

Scroll to our Pledge Section and click the "Submit the Pledge" button to join our community of change-makers. You'll receive a digital badge for your commitment.

Note: No personal data is collected - this is a symbolic commitment to equality.

Engagement Tools

4

Join Our Community

Use the form at the bottom of the page to:

  • Receive monthly myth-busting newsletters
  • Get updates on new role model profiles
  • Access exclusive educational resources
5

Share Your Story

While we don't have an open submission portal yet, you can email us your experiences with gender stereotypes at alpha1.0@gmail.com. Selected stories will be featured!
